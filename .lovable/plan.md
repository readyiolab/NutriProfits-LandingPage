

# Health Business Website Landing Page

## Overview
A modern, trustworthy landing page that sells a "ready-made health business website" — emphasizing simplicity, transparency, and professionalism. Clean design with a calming green/teal color palette conveying health, trust, and growth. AI-generated images throughout for a polished look.

## Design Direction
- **Color palette**: Deep teal/green primary (#0D9488), warm neutrals, soft backgrounds — conveying health, trust, and professionalism
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif with clear hierarchy
- **Layout**: Single-page, fast-loading, minimal and spacious
- **Images**: AI-generated lifestyle/wellness imagery (people working on laptops, healthy living, professional websites on screens)
- **CTA color**: Consistent teal/green button repeated 3x (Hero, Pricing, Final)
